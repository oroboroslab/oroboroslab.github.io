    • Our Mission

    OROBOROS OS was born from a simple belief: powerful AI should be accessible to everyone, without sacrificing privacy or control.

    We envision a world where individuals and organizations can harness the full potential of artificial intelligence on their own terms - where your data stays yours, where AI works for you (not the other way around), and where the benefits of intelligent computing aren't locked behind corporate walls or cloud subscriptions.

    The name "Oroboros" comes from the ancient symbol of a serpent eating its own tail - representing cycles, renewal, and self-sufficiency. Like the symbol, OROBOROS OS is designed to be self-contained and self-sustaining, giving you everything you need without external dependencies.

    Our Core Values

    The principles that guide everything we build

    PRIV

    Privacy First

    Your data is yours. Period. We believe privacy isn't a feature - it's a fundamental right. OROBOROS OS processes everything locally, collects no telemetry, and gives you complete control over your information.

    CTRL

    User Control

    AI should work for you, not control you. We build systems where humans remain in charge. Every AI action can be reviewed, approved, or blocked. You set the boundaries.

    OPEN

    Transparency

    No black boxes, no hidden agendas. We believe in open systems where you can understand exactly what's happening. Audit logs, clear documentation, and honest communication.

    ACCESS

    Accessibility

    Advanced AI shouldn't require a PhD or a corporate budget. We're committed to making powerful AI tools accessible to individuals, small businesses, and organizations of all sizes.

    Level 99 Foundation

    OROBOROS OS is developed by the Level 99 Foundation, an organization dedicated to advancing accessible technology solutions.

    Our team combines expertise in artificial intelligence, systems architecture, security, and user experience design. We're passionate about building tools that empower users rather than exploit them.

    The foundation operates on the principle that technology should serve humanity's interests. We're committed to ethical AI development, transparent practices, and putting users first in everything we create.

    Why OROBOROS OS?

    What makes us different

    No Cloud Dependency

    Unlike cloud-based AI services, OROBOROS OS runs entirely on your hardware. No internet required for core features. No subscription fees. No data leaving your device.

    True Ownership

    You own the software, the models, and your data. No "service can be terminated at any time" clauses. No vendor lock-in. Your AI environment belongs to you.

    Dual-AI Innovation

    Our unique 2AI architecture provides capabilities no single-AI system can match. Built-in verification, error checking, and quality assurance through AI collaboration.

    Community-Driven

    We listen to our users and build what they need. Feature requests, bug reports, and community contributions shape the direction of OROBOROS OS.
